Know More About Your HSRP Number Plate

What is an HSRP number Plate?

A High-Security Registration Plate (HSRP) is a tamper-proof number plate made of aluminum. It has several security features, including a chromium-based hologram of the Ashoka Chakra, a laser-etched 10-digit PIN, and hot-stamped film applied over the registration number.

Why are HSRP number plate necessary?

HSRP number plates have several benefits, including:

  • They are difficult to counterfeit or tamper with, which helps to deter vehicle theft and other crimes.
  • They make it easier to identify vehicles, which can help law enforcement authorities to track down stolen vehicles and enforce traffic laws.
  • They are also more reflective than traditional number plates, which makes vehicles more visible at night and in low-light conditions.

Are HSRP number plates mandatory in India?

Yes, HSRP number plates are mandatory for all vehicles in India, including new and old vehicles. The deadline for all vehicles to have HSRP number plates was July 2022, but some states have extended the deadline.

How much does an HSRP number plate cost?

The cost of an HSRP number plate varies depending on the type of vehicle you have. For example, HSRP number plates for two-wheelers are typically less expensive than HSRP number plates for four-wheelers.

Can I change the color of my HSRP number plate?

In general, the color of HSRP number plates is standardized and may vary by the type of vehicle. It’s advisable to check with local authorities or the HSRP issuing agency for specific guidelines on color changes.
